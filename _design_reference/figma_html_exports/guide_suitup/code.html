How to Measure

Follow these steps to ensure a perfect fit for your bespoke garment.

1

The Neck

Wrap the tape measure around the base of your neck, leaving room for two fingers to ensure a comfortable collar fit.

2

The Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level under your arms and across your back.

3

The Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, roughly at the level of your navel. Do not suck in your stomach.
